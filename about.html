About Us

Our Background

Learn and Grow Initiative was founded in 2019 to support rural children academically while exploring their creative abilities.

Our Foundation

We are built by the principle of hardworking, empowerment and education for all children.

Our Mission

To provide good quality education and learning resources, tutoring, after school classes and creative opportunities.

Our Vision

A future and a world where every child, regardless of background disadvantages can reach their full potential.

Our Team

Committed volunteers, teachers, sponsors and community members work together to create an opportunity for every learner.
